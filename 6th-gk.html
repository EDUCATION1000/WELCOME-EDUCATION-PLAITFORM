Chapter 1: Introduction to MATH

Science is the systematic study of the structure and behavior of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment. It helps us understand how the world around us works.

Importance of Science:

  • Helps in technological development
  • Improves quality of life
  • Solves real-world problems

Branches of Science:

The main branches are Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Each deals with different aspects of the natural world.
